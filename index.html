WELCOME

We GDSC CRCE and HAWK-i CRCE are absolutely thrilled to announce an event for SYNERGY 2022 in accordance with the Student Council!

- Rules

  • ONLY 2 TO 4 MEMBERS ARE ALLOWED IN A TEAM.
  • PARTICIPANTS SHOULD FILL UP THE REGISTRATION FORM BEFORE THE DEADLINE THROUGH THE REGISTRATION LINK.
  • PARTICIPANTS SHOULD CARRY THEIR IDENTITY CARD AT THE DAY OF EVENT
  • IN CASE OF ANY CONFLICTS THE DECISION OF THE JUDGES WILL BE FINAL FOR THE CTFS.
  • ANY MISCONDUCT/MISBEHAVIOR WILL LEAD TO DISQUALIFICATION OF TEAM.
  • PARTICIPANTS SHOULD NOT SHARE THEIR FLAGS WITH OTHER TEAM STUDENTS AS THIS WILL LEAD TO DISQUALIFICATION.
  • INITIALLY EVERYONE WILL HAVE 4 SECTIONS I.E 4 CTFS TO SOLVE.
  • STUDENTS ARE ALLOWED TO USE GOOGLE ON THE ALLOCATED DEVICE ONLY
  • EACH TEAM WILL HAVE ONE MODERATOR AND ONE COUNCIL MEMBER TO VIEW THEIR ACTIVITIES AND GUIDE THEM IF NEEDED.
  • EACH TEAM HAS TO SOLVE ALL THE 4 CTFâ€™S AND SUBMIT ALL 4 FLAGS WITHIN THE ALLOTTED TIME INSIDE THE WEBSITE.
  • IF STUDENTS ARE NOT ABLE TO SOLVE ANY CTF THEN THEY CAN USE THE HINTS (3 HINTS MAX) BUT WHILE USING THE HINTS THEIR TIME WILL BE REDUCED AS FOLLOWS:
    • 1ST HINT -> MINUS 15 SEC
    • 2ND HINT -> MINUS 30 SEC
    • 3RD HINT -> MINUS 60 SEC


-Rules for Flag

  • EVERY TEAM IS EXPECTED TO COLLECT FLAGS WITHIN THE TIME ALLOTTED
  • USE OF MOBILE PHONE WILL BE RESTRICTED DURING THE COURSE OF THE EVENT.
  • REPORTING TIME: 10 MINS PRIOR TO SCHEDULED EVENT TIME.
  • STUDENTS ARE ALLOWED TO GOOGLE TO SEARCH REGARDING THE HINTS AND FLAGS TO GET THE FLAG BUT
  • IF FOUND DOING ANYTHING ELSE EXCEPT THE ABOVE POINT THEN THEY WILL BE DIRECTLY ELIMINATED.


Judging Criteria

  • WINNER WOULD BE DECIDED ON THE BASIS OF THE NUMBER OF FLAGS COLLECTED AND TIME TAKEN TO COLLECT EACH FLAG. IF TWO TEAMS COLLECT SAME NUMBER OF FLAGS IN SAME TIME THEN THEN NO. OF HINTS WILL BE TAKEN INTO CONSIDERATION TO FIND THE WINNER
  • DECISION OF THE JUDGES WOULD BE EVALUATED WITH THE REMARKS BY THE MODERATORS AND THEN FINAL CALL WILL BE TAKEN BY THE COUNCIL.
